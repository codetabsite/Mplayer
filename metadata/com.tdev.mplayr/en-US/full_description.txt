MPlayer is a minimal, open-source MP3 player for Android with no ads and no bloat.

Features:
- Clean, minimal interface
- Waveform seek bar
- Favorites support
- Sleep timer
- Lyrics editor (LRC format)
- Duplicate song cleaner
- Folder blacklist
- Trash / recycle bin for deleted songs
- Ringtone maker
- Stats and play history
- Album art with palette-based theming
- Headset controls
- MediaSession / notification controls
